About Us

Full Heart Farm , is an enhanced rural association situated in Kality that works in giving top notch table eggs to the Toledo, Denver region and encompassing networks, just as to business sectors situated in the eastern United States. Throughout the years Full Heart Farm Poultry has kept up a notoriety for being one of Ethiopia's driving makers of top notch eggs. Begun in 1959 and fused in 1966, Full Heart Farm Poultry has developed from a family ranch to a completely incorporated organization that controls all periods of the item cycle. The on location handling office has been an enormous supporter of the accomplishment of Full Heart Farm Poultry. It, alongside different aspects of the organization, has helped Full Heart Farm Poultry continue on and thrive for as long as 45 years.

Our Story

For more than 75 years, the the poultry family have pioneered poultry innovation in Ethiopia. Years of hard work and experience has seen us grow into one of our country’s largest poultry producers. Our mission is to inspire Ethiopia to eat well and live well. We’re committed to bringing families together through high quality produce, and strive to support our community to live their best lives possible through a nourishing diet. Our high quality products are distributed to supermarkets, wholesalers, food service distributors and farmers across our country.

Vision

Our vision is to see a developed Ethiopian poultry sub sector where our members are prosper and empowered economically and become competitive internationally. To promote Ethiopian poultry sub sector through advocating for favorable business environments, better technology introduction to its members, creating access to training and other capacity building activities to its members and networking with other similar associations. Google’s mission statement: “Our mission is to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful.” Walt Disney’s mission: “The mission of The Walt Disney Company is to entertain, inform and inspire people around the globe through the power of unparalleled storytelling, reflecting the iconic brands, creative minds and innovative technologies that make ours the world’s premier entertainment company.” So, let’s repeat the key difference. A vision statement is a definition of a company’s future, while a mission statement represents a roadmap that tells what the company will do to achieve its vision.
